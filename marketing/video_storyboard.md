## Production Notes

### Visual Style
- **Color Palette**: Primary: #FF6B00 (Orange), Secondary: #FF9E00 (Yellow), Neutral: #212529 (Dark Gray)
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif font (Montserrat or similar)
- **Interface Style**: Modern, minimal, with subtle shadows and clean white space
- **Animations**: Smooth, professional transitions between scenes
- **Branding**: Consistent .fylr logo placement in corner

### Voice-Over Guidance
- **Tone**: Professional but friendly and conversational
- **Pace**: Moderate, allowing viewers to absorb information
- **Gender**: Either male or female, but consistent throughout
- **Accent**: Neutral, clearly understandable

### Music Recommendation
- Upbeat, modern instrumental that conveys efficiency and professionalism
- Should build slightly during key features and call-to-action
- No lyrics to compete with voice-over

### Screen Recording Guidelines
- Use high-resolution (1080p minimum) screen captures
- Highlight cursor movements and clicks
- Use zoom-in effects for important details
- Keep interactions smooth and error-free
- Use .fylr demo account with realistic but non-sensitive sample data

### B-Roll Suggestions
- Business owners working on laptops in modern spaces
- Close-ups of relieved expressions when completing tasks
- Brief shots of traditional tax paperwork for contrast
- Casual office environments with entrepreneurs

### Caption Requirements
- Include accurate closed captions for accessibility
- Consider adding text overlays for key points regardless of captions
- Use consistent positioning and styling for all text elements

### Compliance Considerations
- Include appropriate disclaimers throughout
- Ensure all sample data is fictitious but realistic
- Avoid making specific savings claims without qualification
- Clearly distinguish between plan features
