# .fylr New User Guide

## Welcome to .fylr!

This guide will walk you through the process of getting started with .fylr, your AI-powered tax automation platform. We've designed this to be as straightforward as possible, so you can focus on your business, not your taxes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. **Sign up** by entering your email and creating a password
2. **Verify your email** by clicking the link we send you
3. **Complete your profile** with your basic information

## Step 2: Tell Us About Your Business

1. **Enter your business name** and contact information
2. **Select your business type**:
   - Sole Proprietorship
   - LLC (Single-member or Multi-member)
   - S Corporation
   - C Corporation

3. **Indicate your industry** from our dropdown menu
4. **Provide basic financial information**:
   - Approximate annual revenue
   - Number of employees (if any)
   - States where you operate

## Step 3: Connect Your Financial Data

### Option A: Upload Documents
1. **Drag and drop** your financial documents to our secure uploader
2. Our **OCR technology automatically extracts** key information
3. Documents are automatically **categorized and organized**

### Option B: Connect Accounting Software
1. **Select your accounting platform** (QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, etc.)
2. **Authorize the connection** securely (we never store your credentials)
3. **Select the data** you want to import:
   - Chart of accounts
   - Profit & loss statements
   - Balance sheets
   - Transactions

## Step 4: Complete the AI Questionnaire

1. **Answer simple questions** in plain language
2. Questions are **tailored to your business type**
3. Each response helps our AI better understand your tax situation
4. **Save progress** at any time and return later

## Step 5: Review Your Pre-Filled Forms

1. Our AI **selects the appropriate forms** for your business type
2. Forms are **pre-filled with your information**
3. **Yellow highlights** indicate fields needing your attention
4. **Tooltips provide guidance** for complex fields

## Step 6: Explore Tax Strategies

1. View **AI-generated tax strategy recommendations**
2. Strategies are **tailored to your business**
3. **Implementation steps** guide you through each strategy
4. **Save or export** strategies for future reference

## Step 7: Export Your Tax Documents

1. **Review your completed forms**
2. **Choose your export format**:
   - PDF (for printing and review)
   - JSON (for data storage)
   - HTML (for sharing via email)
3. **Include any supporting documents** in your package

## Tips for Success

1. **Complete your profile fully** - this helps our AI provide more accurate recommendations
2. **Upload all financial documents** - even if you're unsure if they're relevant
3. **Answer questionnaire honestly** - our AI isn't judging, just optimizing your taxes
