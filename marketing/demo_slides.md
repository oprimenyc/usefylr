## Presentation Notes

### General Guidance
- Present at a moderate pace (approximately 1 minute per slide)
- Be prepared to go into more detail on any slide if questions arise
- Have the live demo environment ready to switch to for interactive demonstrations

### Emphasis Points
- Stress ease of use throughout the presentation
- Highlight time savings compared to traditional methods
- Emphasize the AI's ability to find often-missed deductions
- Point out that the platform grows with the business

### Demo Tips
1. Have a sample business already set up to show the complete flow
2. Pre-upload some sample documents to demonstrate OCR capabilities
3. Show real-time form filling based on questionnaire responses
4. Demonstrate the difference between Basic and Pro strategy recommendations
5. Actually export a document to demonstrate simplicity

### Audience Questions to Anticipate
- "How accurate is the OCR for document processing?"
- "What accounting platforms do you integrate with?"
- "Can I import data from my previous tax preparation service?"
- "How do you protect sensitive financial information?"
- "What happens if I get audited?"

### Follow-up Materials
After the presentation, be prepared to share:
- A link to the onboarding guide
- Sample PDF outputs for different business types
- The video tutorial for new users
- A comparison chart of features by plan level
