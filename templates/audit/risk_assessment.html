Common IRS Audit Triggers
Unreported Income
High Impact

The IRS receives copies of your 1099s and W-2s. Discrepancies between these and your reported income are automated audit triggers.

High Income
Medium Impact

Returns with higher incomes are statistically more likely to be audited, especially those over $200,000.

Large Deductions
Medium Impact

Deductions that are unusually large compared to your income can trigger closer scrutiny.

Home Office Deduction
Medium Impact

This deduction is frequently misused and therefore closely examined by the IRS.

Schedule C Losses
High Impact

Multiple years of business losses on Schedule C may suggest a hobby rather than a business.
