2. Commission Structure

Partner will receive 30% commission on the first year subscription value of each referral, 15% commission on annual renewals, and 10% commission on tier upgrades. Commissions are paid monthly via direct deposit or PayPal.

3. Promotion Guidelines

Partner must clearly disclose the affiliate relationship in all promotions. Partner must not make false claims about the Company or its services. Partner must not engage in spamming or other deceptive marketing practices.

4. Tracking and Reporting

The Company will track all referrals through a unique affiliate link or code. Partner will have access to a dashboard showing clicks, conversions, and commissions earned.

5. Term and Termination

This agreement continues until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice. The Company may terminate immediately if Partner violates any terms of this agreement.

Partner agrees to keep all proprietary information, including commission rates, marketing strategies, and customer data, strictly confidential.
