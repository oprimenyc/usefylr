Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Basic DIY Filing?

Basic DIY Filing includes a simplified tax form generator for businesses with zero or minimal activity. It's perfect for newly formed entities or businesses that were inactive during the tax year.

How does the Guided Filing work?

Guided Filing walks you step-by-step through completing Form 1120, 1065, or Schedule C with explanations of each section and input field. You'll be able to download a completed PDF ready for filing with the IRS.

Do I need accounting experience to use these tools?

No accounting experience is necessary. Our tools are designed to guide you through the process with simple questions and explanations of tax terminology.

What's the Strategy Unlock feature?

Strategy Unlock uses AI technology to analyze your business information and suggest tax-saving strategies tailored to your specific situation, potentially saving you thousands in taxes.

Can I use this for past tax years?

Yes, our tools support tax years from 2019-2025, making them perfect for catching up on unfiled returns or amending previous filings.

What if I'm a Federal Funding Club member?

Federal Funding Club members with the Business Builder plan get full access to all tax tools at no additional cost. Subscription members receive a 50% discount on all tools.
