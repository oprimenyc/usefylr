Overview

This is a modular, AI-assisted tax filing tool for small business owners (C Corps, S Corps, LLCs, Sole Props) who need to file back taxes or simple zero-income returns without hiring a CPA.

It is part of a broader financial services platform offered by FederalFundingClub.com. The tool is designed to:
	•	Generate and fill business tax forms (e.g., 1120, 1065, Schedule C)
	•	Offer IRS letter templates (e.g., first-time abatement, penalty removal)
	•	Provide advanced tax-saving strategies using AI prompts and deduction logic
	•	Sell DIY and done-for-you filing tiers, with discounts for subscription members

⸻

Who It’s For
	•	Business owners with unfiled tax years (2019–2025)
	•	Side hustlers, solopreneurs, and real estate professionals
	•	Anyone wanting to file low-activity or no-income business returns fast
	•	Users seeking IRS penalty relief letters or write-off optimization
	•	Federal Funding Club members with business builder plans

⸻

Key Modules
	•	/modules/form_generator.py – Tax form logic (1120, 1065, etc.)
	•	/modules/strategy_engine.py – Detects possible deductions/credits (QBI, 179, R&D)
	•	/modules/irs_letters.py – Letter builder for late filing and abatement
	•	/ai/analyzer.py – GPT-powered strategy recommender
	•	/app/billing.py – Stripe billing logic with gated feature access
	•	/frontend/ – Clean web UI or dashboard pages
